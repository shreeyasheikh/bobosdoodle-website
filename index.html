About bobosdoodle

Started in 2025, Bobosdoodle offers a curated collection of charming everyday essentials and accessories. Browse our selection of stationery notebooks, hair accessories, mugs, bookmarks, and coin purses – Each piece is designed to bring a little extra joy to your life.

What makes us special?

  • Thoughtfully designed pieces that blend kawaii whimsy with functionality
  • Attention to detail: soft color palettes, gentle shapes, and quality materials
  • A mission to spread positivity, one doodle at a time

Dear friend, thank you for letting Bobosdoodle into your life. May our little creations lift your spirits, brighten your space, and remind you that happiness is found in the small things. 💌
